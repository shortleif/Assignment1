Pokémon Hangman

Test your Pokémon knowledge with this Game Boy-inspired Hangman game based on the original game!

About the Game

Features:

  • Classic Hangman gameplay with Pokémon names
  • All 151 original Pokémon from Generation I
  • Four difficulty levels to test your skills
  • Track your wins, losses, and Pokémon seen
  • Official Pokémon artwork from PokéAPI
  • Retro Game Boy-inspired design
Play Now!

How to Play

  1. Select your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard, or Extreme)
  2. Guess one letter at a time to reveal the Pokémon name
  3. Each incorrect guess reduces your remaining attempts
  4. Try to guess the name before running out of attempts
  5. Press Enter or click "Next Round" to continue
  6. Can you see all 151 original Pokémon?

Difficulty Levels

Easy: 10 attempts

Perfect for beginners or younger players

Medium: 6 attempts

Balanced challenge for casual players

Hard: 3 attempts

For Pokémon fans who know their stuff

Extreme: 1 attempt

True Pokémon masters only!

This project is created for educational purposes.
